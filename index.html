Our Mission

United Men Empowering Brotherhood is dedicated to uplifting and empowering individuals through mentorship and active community service. Our mission is to create strong, supportive bonds within families, recognizing that the foundation of a thriving community starts with nurturing the relationships that shape it.

By fostering a sense of responsibility, brotherhood, and shared purpose, we guide young men to become positive role models, helping them strengthen and support their families, which in turn, contributes to the growth and well-being of the broader community.

Through hands-on involvement, mentorship programs, and collaborative initiatives, we are committed to building a future where every individual has the tools and support needed to succeed and thrive.

Our Programs

Making a difference through action and compassion

Community Fun Day

Annual family-friendly event featuring games, activities, food, and fellowship. Bringing the community together to celebrate unity and joy.

MLK Food Drive

Partnering with Oak Hill Child & Adolescent Center to provide essential groceries and food boxes to families in need throughout the year.

Christmas Toy Giveaway

Spreading holiday joy through toy distributions and gift giving to children and elderly residents at care facilities, making Christmas brighter for all.

Annual Turkey Giveaway

Providing Thanksgiving meals and turkeys to hundreds of families, ensuring everyone can celebrate the holiday with dignity and abundance.

Boys To Men Program

Partnering with DeKalb County Sheriff's Summer Camp, providing mentorship and guidance to help young men develop character and leadership skills.

Brotherhood Mentorship

One-on-one mentorship connecting experienced men with young individuals, fostering personal growth, accountability, and positive life choices.
